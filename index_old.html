Integrated Communcation and Sensing Systems Group
Building next generation of sensors and radios

The Integrated Communication and Sensing Systems Group (ICASS) is a specialized research and development group within the Electrical Engineering Department at KSU. Established in 2022 by Assistant Professor Abdulrahman Alhamed, ICASS is dedicated to exploring cutting-edge phased-array circuit and system advancements for emerging applications in sensing, security, and communication. For a deeper understanding of our work, please visit the Research and Publication sections of our website.

Join Our Team: Each year, we seek talented M.S. and Ph.D. research assistants to join our group. If you're interested in becoming a part of our team, you can apply here. For inquiries regarding postdoctoral positions, please get in touch with Abdulrahman Alhamed directly. Ideal candidates possess backgrounds in analog/mixed-mode/RF circuit design, electromagnetics, or physics, depending on the specific project requirements.
