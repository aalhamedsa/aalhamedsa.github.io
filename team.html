Dr. Abdulrahman Alhamed

Abdulrahman Alhamed is an Assistant Professor in the Electrical Engineering Department at KSU. He received his Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from XYZ University in 2021. His research interests include analog, mixed-mode, and RF circuit design, electromagnetics, and the development of advanced phased-array systems for sensing, security, and communication applications.

Abdulrahman has published numerous articles in prestigious journals and conferences, and he has been the recipient of several awards and honors for his research contributions. He is a member of professional organizations such as IEEE and ACM, and he is actively involved in organizing conferences and workshops in his field.

Outside of his academic work, Abdulrahman enjoys spending time with his family, traveling, and exploring new technologies. He is also an avid sports enthusiast and enjoys playing soccer and basketball.
